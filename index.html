Software businesses rely on.

I'm a full-stack engineer and the Head of Operations at SecurePath Technologies in Nairobi, where I lead the design and delivery of products used at national scale — from secure document and certificate systems to e-marking platforms.

I work across the whole stack — Java & Spring Boot on the backend, React & Next.js on the front, plus Android, desktop and cloud. Holding a CEH v11, I bring a security-first mindset to everything I ship.

Outside of SecurePath, I'm building Convero — an omnichannel AI sales & voice platform — and Alama, an offline-first school-assessment app for Kenya's CBE system.
